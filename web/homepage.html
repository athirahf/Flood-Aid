ABOUT US

Flood Aid is a non-governmental organization (NGO) based in Malaysia, dedicated to improving the lives of Malaysians affected by natural disasters, particularly floods. The organization's primary mission is to provide immediate support to flood victims and enhance the overall disaster rescue processes.Flood Aid is driven by a passionate team of volunteers who are committed to giving back to the community, especially those in need and underprivileged.

OUR PLEDGE TO FLOOD VICTIM

This initiative by Flood Aid focuses on providing immediate support to flood victims in Malaysia through the development of a web application called "Banjir Rescue." The app acts as a vital link between those impacted by floods and the resources they urgently need, including shelters and volunteers. By facilitating these connections, Flood Aid aims to strengthen community resilience in the face of natural disasters.
